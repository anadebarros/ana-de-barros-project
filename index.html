About Me

I was born in Lisbon at the time Gameboy was a great thing. Still is. I've always loved many things but my path was divided between music and science. I graduated in Genetics and received my PhD in immunology. I then discovered web development and it just felt natural: I could finally connect arts and science into one thing I love to do! Currently finishing a Web and Mobile Development course @FLAG, I can't wait to do this for a living!
